# Tokenomics
2. Token design overview

Token type:  
- Standard: ERC‑20 with governance extensions (e.g., ERC20Votes)  
- Chain: Ethereum mainnet  

Core functions:  
- Governance: Vote on proposals, budgets, upgrades, partnerships.  
- Utility: Access tiers, staking, rewards, and internal payments.  
- Reputation: Basis for non-transferable participation badges or scores.

---

3. Tokenomics

Total supply:  
- Fixed: \(100,000,000\) GST

Distribution:

| Category                     | Allocation | Description                                 |
|-----------------------------|-----------:|---------------------------------------------|
| Community & Airdrops        | 35%        | Early adopters, contributors, testers       |
| Ecosystem Utility Rewards   | 25%        | Staking, liquidity mining, participation    |
| Treasury                    | 20%        | Grants, development, strategic initiatives  |
| Team & Founders             | 15%        | Long-term aligned, fully vested over time   |
| Liquidity                   | 5%         | Initial DEX liquidity (e.g., Uniswap)      |

Vesting and lockups:

- Team & founders:  
  - Cliff: 12 months  
  - Vesting: Linear over 36 months  
- Treasury:  
  - Controlled by governance, with timelock for all large disbursements.  
- Rewards:  
  - Emitted over 5–8 years via on-chain schedules.
