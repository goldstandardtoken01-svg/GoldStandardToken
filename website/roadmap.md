# Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Foundation
- Repository setup and community building
- Draft whitepaper, tokenomics, and governance documents

## Phase 1 — Launch
- Deploy initial token contracts
- Conduct security audits
- Begin reserve formation and custodian agreements

## Phase 2 — Growth
- Integrations with major wallets and DEXs
- Ecosystem grants and partnerships

## Phase 3 — Maturity
- Full DAO handover of governance
- Ongoing reserve reporting and audits
