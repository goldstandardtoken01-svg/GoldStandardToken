# FAQ

Q: What is GST?
A: Gold Standard Token (GST) is a token backed by diversified real-world reserves and governed by a DAO.

Q: How many tokens exist?
A: Initial example supply is 1,000,000 GST; actual supply and issuance rules are determined by the DAO.

Q: How can I participate in governance?
A: Hold GST and follow the governance process to submit or vote on proposals.

Q: Where are reserves held?
A: Reserve custodians and allocation details will be published and audited; this information will be posted in the repository and public reports.

Q: Is GST audited?
A: Smart contracts and reserve attestations will be audited by third parties; audit reports will be published.
